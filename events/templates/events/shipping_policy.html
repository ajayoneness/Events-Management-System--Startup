Ticket Delivery Policy

Last updated: June 2025  |  Effective from: January 2024

Instant Digital Delivery
All EventHub tickets are digital. No physical shipping required. Your ticket is delivered instantly to your email after payment confirmation.

EventHub is a 100% digital ticketing platform. We do not sell or ship any physical goods. All tickets are delivered electronically. This policy explains how and when you will receive your tickets after a successful booking.

How Ticket Delivery Works

1
Complete Payment

After selecting your tickets and entering attendee details, complete payment via Razorpay (UPI, card, netbanking, wallet).

2
Instant Booking Confirmation

Upon successful payment, your booking is confirmed immediately and a unique QR code ticket is generated.

3
Email Delivery

Your ticket (with QR code) is emailed to the address you provided at booking. Check your spam/junk folder if you don't receive it within 15 minutes.

4
WhatsApp Notification (if enabled)

Depending on the event, a booking confirmation may also be sent to your WhatsApp number.

5
Download Anytime

Log in to your account and visit My Tickets to download or view your ticket at any time, even without the email.

Delivery Channels

  • Email — Ticket with QR code sent to your registered email address.
  • Account Dashboard — Always accessible at My Tickets after login.
  • Booking Confirmation Page — Available immediately after payment.
  • WhatsApp — Optional notification to your phone number (event-specific).

Delivery Timeframes

  • Email confirmation: Within 5–15 minutes of successful payment.
  • Account access: Immediate — available as soon as payment is confirmed.
  • Delayed emails: If you don't receive your email within 30 minutes, please check spam and contact us.

Ticket Access at Event

  • Present your QR code (on phone or printed) at the event entry gate.
  • Event staff will scan your QR code using EventHub's verification system.
  • Each QR code is valid for single-use entry only.
  • Ensure your phone screen brightness is adequate for scanning.

Lost or Missing Tickets

If you cannot find your ticket:

  • Log in to your EventHub account and visit My Tickets to download it again.
  • Use Find My Booking with your booking ID or email — no login required.
  • Contact us at support@eventhub.in with your booking ID and registered email.

No Physical Shipping

EventHub does not ship any physical products. If you are expecting a physical ticket, wristband, or merchandise, please contact the event Organiser directly. EventHub only handles digital ticket delivery.

No physical goods are sold or shipped by EventHub. All products are digital tickets delivered electronically.
