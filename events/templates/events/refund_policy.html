1. Marketplace Disclosure

Passly Hai is a technology platform and marketplace connecting event organizers and attendees.

Passly Hai does not organize, host, manage, or control events listed on the platform.

Refund obligations remain the responsibility of the respective event organizer.

2. No Refund After Successful Booking

Except as expressly stated in this policy or required by applicable law, all ticket purchases are final.

Once a booking is successfully completed, tickets cannot be:

  • Cancelled
  • Exchanged
  • Modified
  • Refunded

due to personal reasons, scheduling conflicts, change of plans, inability to attend, travel issues, or any other attendee-related circumstances.

3. Event Cancellation

If an event is completely cancelled by the organizer and is not rescheduled, attendees may be eligible for a refund as determined by the organizer.

Refund requests must be submitted within the period specified by the organizer.

4. Non-Refundable Charges

Even where a refund is approved, the following charges may be non-refundable:

  • Passly Hai Platform Fees
  • Convenience Fees
  • Payment Gateway Charges
  • Processing Charges
  • Taxes collected on such fees where applicable
  • Any fixed Passly Hai service charges

Only the ticket face value approved by the organizer may be eligible for refund.

5. Organizer Responsibility

The organizer is solely responsible for:

  • Approving refunds
  • Processing refund requests
  • Determining refund eligibility
  • Communicating refund timelines

Passly Hai may facilitate communication but is not obligated to provide refunds from its own funds.

6. Organizer Contact Information

To assist with refund requests and dispute resolution, Passly Hai may provide organizer contact information to attendees and may share attendee information with organizers.

7. Postponed or Rescheduled Events

If an event is postponed or rescheduled:

  • Existing tickets may remain valid.
  • Refund eligibility shall be determined solely by the organizer.
  • Passly Hai does not guarantee refunds for postponed events.

8. Fraudulent or Duplicate Transactions

Passly Hai reserves the right to cancel bookings suspected of fraud, abuse, unauthorized payment activity, or duplication.

9. Refund Processing Time

Where refunds are approved by organizers, processing may take 7–15 business days or longer depending on payment providers, banks, and payment methods.
