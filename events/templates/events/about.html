Our Story

Making Events Unforgettable

Passly Hai is India's growing event discovery and ticketing platform, built to connect passionate event organisers with audiences who love live experiences.

Our Mission

Bringing People Together Through Shared Experiences

We believe that live events — concerts, workshops, conferences, cultural festivals — have the power to create memories that last a lifetime. Passly Hai exists to make discovering, booking, and attending these experiences effortless.

For organisers, we provide the tools to manage events, sell tickets, track sales, and communicate with attendees — all in one place, with zero technical complexity.

Founded in 2024
Started with a vision to make ticketing simple, transparent, and attendee-first for events across India.

Our Values
