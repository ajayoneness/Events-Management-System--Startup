Organizer Agreement

Last Updated: June 2026

This Organizer Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Passly Hai ("Passly Hai", "Platform", "we", "our", or "us") and the individual, business, organization, institution, promoter, or entity ("Organizer") using Passly Hai to create, promote, manage, or sell tickets for events. By creating an organizer account, publishing an event, or using any organizer services, you agree to be bound by this Agreement.

1. Platform Relationship

Passly Hai operates solely as a technology platform and marketplace that facilitates event ticketing and event management services.

Passly Hai is not:

  • Event organizer
  • Event promoter
  • Event sponsor
  • Venue owner
  • Event manager
  • Event operator

The Organizer remains solely responsible for the planning, management, operation, and execution of all events listed on the Platform.

2. Organizer Eligibility

The Organizer represents and warrants that:

  • All information provided is accurate and complete.
  • The Organizer has authority to conduct and promote the event.
  • All required licenses, permissions, approvals, and permits have been obtained.
  • The event complies with applicable laws and regulations.

Passly Hai may request identity verification, business verification, GST information, PAN information, bank account verification, or other documentation at any time.

3. Event Responsibility

The Organizer shall be solely responsible for:

  • Event execution and safety
  • Event staff and venue arrangements
  • Crowd management and security
  • Emergency planning
  • Event content and schedules
  • Artist or speaker management
  • Government permissions
  • Compliance with applicable laws

Passly Hai shall have no responsibility for any aspect of event operations.

4. Event Listings

The Organizer must ensure that all event information is accurate, including:

  • Event name, date and time, venue details
  • Ticket pricing and capacity limits
  • Refund policies, age restrictions, entry requirements

The Organizer must immediately update any incorrect information.

5. Prohibited Events

The Organizer may not use Passly Hai for:

  • Fraudulent events or illegal activities
  • Gambling activities or pyramid schemes
  • Hate speech events or terrorist activities
  • Adult or unlawful content
  • Events violating Indian law

Passly Hai may suspend or remove such events without notice.

6. Ticket Sales and Payments

Passly Hai may collect payments on behalf of the Organizer through authorized payment partners.

The Organizer authorizes Passly Hai to:

  • Collect ticket payments
  • Deduct applicable fees and taxes where required
  • Deduct chargebacks or penalties where applicable
  • Transfer eligible payouts to the Organizer

Passly Hai does not guarantee any minimum ticket sales.

7. Platform Fees

The Organizer agrees that Passly Hai may charge:

  • Platform Fees, Service Fees, Convenience Fees
  • Payment Gateway Charges
  • Payout Processing Fees
  • Other disclosed charges

These fees may be deducted before payout. All Passly Hai fees are non-refundable.

8. Refund Responsibility

The Organizer acknowledges and agrees that:

  • The Organizer is solely responsible for all event refunds.
  • The Organizer determines refund eligibility.
  • The Organizer is responsible for communicating refund decisions to attendees.
  • The Organizer shall bear all approved refund costs unless otherwise agreed.

Passly Hai acts only as a technology intermediary.

9. Event Cancellation

If an event is cancelled, postponed, rescheduled, abandoned, or materially changed, the Organizer shall:

  • Notify Passly Hai immediately
  • Notify attendees promptly
  • Honor applicable refund commitments
  • Cooperate with customer support inquiries

Passly Hai may suspend payouts until refund obligations are satisfied.

10. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

If attendees initiate chargebacks, payment disputes, reversals, or fraud claims related to the Organizer's event:

  • Passly Hai may withhold payouts
  • Passly Hai may deduct disputed amounts from future payouts
  • Passly Hai may recover losses from the Organizer

The Organizer remains financially responsible for chargeback-related losses arising from their events.

11. Attendee Data Access

The Organizer may receive attendee information including Name, Mobile Number, Email Address, and Ticket Information solely for event-related purposes.

12. Attendee Data Restrictions

The Organizer shall not:

  • Sell or rent attendee data
  • Share attendee data with unrelated third parties
  • Use attendee data for unauthorized marketing
  • Transfer attendee data outside legitimate event operations

13. Data Retention Requirement

The Organizer must permanently delete attendee personal information obtained through Passly Hai within three (3) months after completion of the event unless retention is legally required.

Passly Hai may request confirmation of compliance. Violation may result in:

  • Immediate suspension
  • Permanent account termination
  • Legal action
  • Withholding of payouts

14. Organizer Public Profile

The Organizer authorizes Passly Hai to publicly display Organizer Name, Username, Logo, Event Listings, Public Contact Information, and Social Media Links for platform and marketing purposes.

15. Refund Assistance

Where attendees request refunds or event support, Passly Hai may share attendee details with the Organizer, share Organizer support contact information with attendees, and facilitate communication between parties.

16. Intellectual Property

The Organizer grants Passly Hai a worldwide, royalty-free license to use event names, descriptions, logos, images, videos, and promotional materials for platform operations, marketing, and promotional purposes.

17. Indemnification

The Organizer agrees to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Passly Hai, its owners, employees, contractors, partners, and affiliates from any claims, losses, damages, liabilities, penalties, costs, or expenses arising from: event cancellation or postponement, venue issues, personal injury, property damage, regulatory or copyright violations, attendee disputes, refund disputes, or organizer misconduct.

18. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, Passly Hai shall not be liable for lost profits, lost revenue, event failure, event attendance levels, business interruption, indirect damages, or consequential damages.

Passly Hai's total liability shall not exceed the platform fees received from the relevant event.

19. Suspension and Termination

Passly Hai may suspend, restrict, delay payouts, remove events, or terminate organizer accounts at any time where fraud is suspected, legal violations occur, data misuse occurs, chargeback risk exists, safety concerns arise, or platform policies are violated.

20. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

21. Jurisdiction

Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts located in Uttar Pradesh, India.
