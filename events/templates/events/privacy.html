Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Passly Hai ("Passly Hai", "Passly", "we", "our", or "us") operates an online event ticketing and event management marketplace that connects event organizers and attendees. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, and share your information when you use PasslyHai.com and related services.

1. Information We Collect

When using Passly Hai, we may collect:

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • City and State

Event Registration Information

  • Transaction Information
  • Ticket Purchase Details
  • Booking Information
  • Order History
  • Payment Status
  • Refund Status

Technical Information

  • IP Address
  • Device Information
  • Browser Information
  • Website Usage Data
  • Cookies and Analytics Information

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Process ticket bookings
  • Generate tickets and QR codes
  • Verify event attendance
  • Send booking confirmations
  • Provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve platform services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Information with Event Organizers

When you purchase a ticket through Passly Hai, you expressly consent to the sharing of your:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Ticket Details
  • Booking Information

with the organizer of the event for which the ticket is purchased.

This information is shared solely for:

  • Event entry and verification
  • Attendee management
  • Event-related communication
  • Customer support
  • Safety and compliance purposes

4. Organizer Data Retention Rules

Event organizers may access attendee information only for their own events.

Organizers are prohibited from:

  • Selling attendee information
  • Sharing attendee information with unrelated third parties
  • Using attendee information for unauthorized marketing
  • Storing attendee information beyond permitted periods

Organizers must delete attendee personal information obtained through Passly Hai within three (3) months after completion of the event unless required by applicable law.

Passly Hai may suspend or permanently remove organizers who violate these requirements.

5. Refund Related Information Sharing

If a user requests a refund, cancellation, dispute resolution, or event-related assistance, Passly Hai may share relevant information between the attendee and organizer to facilitate communication and resolution.

Passly Hai may also provide organizer contact information to attendees where necessary for refund or event-related support.

6. Third-Party Service Providers

We may share information with trusted service providers including:

  • Payment Gateways
  • SMS Providers
  • Email Providers
  • Cloud Hosting Services
  • Analytics Providers
  • Security and Fraud Prevention Providers

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

8. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information where permitted by applicable law by contacting us at:

Email: contact@passlyhai.com

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
